Video Chat

Connect instantly with friends anywhere. Create a private room, invite others with a link or code, and jump into real-time video for party games like charades, trivia, and more.

How It Works

1) Create a Room: Start a new room and get a shareable invite.
2) Invite Friends: Send the room link or code to your group.
3) Join & Play: Turn on your camera/mic and start your game session in seconds!

Features

• Private rooms for group calls.
• Real time audio and video communication.
• Lightweight interface that works great on desktop, and on mobile devices.
• Profile page to manage your account details.
• Built with a focus on speed and smooth user experience.

Privacy & Safety

Your camera and microphone are only accessed with your permission. For the best experienece, use a trusted network and only share room invites with people you know.

This application was develooped for a unit project at Base Camp Coding Academy It demonstrates full-stack skills including authentication, user profiles, and real-time communication.

Team Members: Jonathan Tutor, Bryson Flowers, Lemuel Tzib
